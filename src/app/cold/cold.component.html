Cold Observable Demo

Cold observables don't begin emitting values until you subscribe to them. Each subscription gets all of the observable's data regardless of when the subscription starts.

In both counters, the second subscription is started after 2.5 seconds. In the cold counter, both subscriptions start at 1 and get all of the observable's data from the beginning. In the hot counter, the second subscription simply joins in to the feed that the first subscription is getting.

The common analogy for cold observables is a prerecorded video: it can be watched by different people at different times, and they would each see the same thing.
