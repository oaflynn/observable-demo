Hot Observable Demo: Keydown

Hot observables are streams of events that occur whether or not one is subscribed to them or not. You can keydown before and after subscribing, but nothing will happen because nothing is listening for the keydowns.

Hot observables can also have multiple subscriptions that start at different points and only recieve data for the time that they are subscribed. This is demonstrated if you begin the subscription, do some keydowns, then add the second subscriptions and do more keydowns.

A common analogy for hot observables is a live performance: everyone watching is seeing the same thing at the same exact time. If someone comes late, they only see the part they are there for.
